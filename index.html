Seattle Community Cellular Network

The Local Connectivity Lab (LCL) is a 501(c)(3) registered non-profit in partnership with the University of Washington (UW) to share free or low-cost broadband access in higher-need areas throughout the city, making use of existing UW and city network infrastructure such as buildings and fiber-optic cables to extend coverage to more people.

About Us

The mission of the Local Connectivity Lab (LCL) is to facilitate community focused technology development and research in support of low-income, marginalized populations and groups. LCL is a non-profit that aims to work with communities to build sustainable community relations and to provide Internet to community via community networks maintained by the communities they serve.
